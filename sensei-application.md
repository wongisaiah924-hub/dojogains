# Sensei Mentorship Application

## Flow
1. Applicant fills out form
2. You review application
3. If qualified, you email them to schedule Google Meet
4. After call, if good fit, you send them Whop payment link

---

## Application Questions (for Google Form)

**Title:** Dojo Gains Sensei Mentorship Application

**Description:** 
Thank you for your interest in the Sensei Mentorship program. Please answer the following questions so we can determine if the program is a good fit for you. Serious inquiries only — limited spots available.

---

### Questions:

1. **Full Name** (Short answer - Required)

2. **Email Address** (Short answer - Required)

3. **What's your trading experience level?** (Multiple choice - Required)
   - Complete beginner (less than 6 months)
   - Some experience (6 months - 2 years)
   - Intermediate (2-5 years)
   - Advanced (5+ years)

4. **What markets do you currently trade or want to trade?** (Checkboxes)
   - ES / MES Futures
   - NQ / MNQ Futures
   - Options
   - Stocks
   - Forex
   - Crypto
   - Other

5. **Are you currently funded or pursuing a prop firm?** (Multiple choice)
   - Yes, currently funded
   - In evaluation/challenge
   - Planning to start soon
   - Not interested in prop firms

6. **What's your biggest trading challenge right now?** (Paragraph - Required)

7. **Why are you interested in 1-on-1 mentorship?** (Paragraph - Required)

8. **What are your trading goals for the next 6-12 months?** (Paragraph - Required)

9. **How many hours per week can you dedicate to learning and trading?** (Multiple choice)
   - Less than 5 hours
   - 5-10 hours
   - 10-20 hours
   - 20+ hours

10. **Are you prepared to invest in yourself and commit to the mentorship program?** (Multiple choice - Required)
    - Yes, I'm ready to commit
    - I need more information first

11. **How did you hear about Dojo Gains?** (Short answer)

12. **Anything else you'd like us to know?** (Paragraph - Optional)

---
