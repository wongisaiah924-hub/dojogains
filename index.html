FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a signal service?

No. The Dojo teaches you to find your own setups and make your own decisions. We build traders, not followers.

I'm a complete beginner. Can I join?

Absolutely. Every master was once a white belt. The training starts from fundamentals and the community welcomes all levels.

What markets do you train?

Primarily futures (ES, NQ) and options. The principles apply across markets, but our live sessions focus on these.

How is this different from other groups?

No hype. No guru worship. No promises of easy money. Just disciplined training, honest feedback, and a community focused on mastery.

Can I leave anytime?

Yes. Monthly memberships can be canceled anytime. We also offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. No hard feelings.

How much time do I need to commit?

Train at your own pace. The Dojo is open 24/7. Live sessions happen during market hours. Show up when you can.
