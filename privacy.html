Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly:

  • Account information: Email address and name when you sign up through Whop
  • Discord information: Your Discord username when you connect to our community
  • Contact form submissions: Name, email, and message content when you reach out
  • Application information: Details you provide when applying for mentorship

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide access to membership content and community
  • Process payments (handled by Whop)
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send important updates about your membership
  • Improve our educational content and services

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Whop: Handles payments and membership management. See Whop's Privacy Policy
  • Discord: Hosts our community. See Discord's Privacy Policy
  • FormSubmit: Processes contact form submissions
  • GitHub Pages: Hosts this website

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Payment information is handled entirely by Whop — we never see or store your card details.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Request we correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request we delete your data
  • Unsubscribe: Opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us.

6. Cookies

This website uses minimal cookies for basic functionality. Third-party embeds (like Whop or Discord widgets) may use their own cookies according to their policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Dojo Gains is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated through our Discord or email.

9. Contact

Questions about your privacy? Contact us.
